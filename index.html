Aromatherapy is complementary therapy and alternative medicine that incorporates several essential plant extracts and oils to ease the mind and body.

Our corporation provides aromatherapy as a service with a team of aromatherapy enthusiasts that specialise in physical therapy and incorporate different types of aroma oils and essential oils in their sessions.

Register to our services to book an appointment with our aromatherapists and visit our aromatic studios to purchase our aromatherapy accessories that are only available to our clients.

You can choose the body oils you prefer to be used in your skin. The primary body oils that we use in our studios are, olive oil, jojoba oil, and coconut oil.

If you have any allergies to any essential oils, inform us and we will provide you with information of essential oils and aroma oils we use in our sessions. We recommend you consult your doctor first before participating in our services if you have any allergies.

About Us

Aromatherapay helps promote relaxation, alleviate stress and encourage lighter mood swings. We use several different essential oils that we mix with our aroma oils that provide different benefits and give your mind and body a unique calming experiences.

Lavender oils: the most popular scent and common oil used in bueaty and cosmetic products. It is recommended to assuage stress and anxiety.

Tea tree oil: This medicinal oil is used in clinical aromatherapies to help with acne, ringworms and athlete's foot.

Frankincense oil: Helps with inflammations and is typically found in skin creams.

Peppermint oil: Based on the National library of medicine, their report states that peppermint oil has an anti-inflammatory, antifungal and antimicrobial properties. It is used in our studio to fight body fatigue which typically lifts mood.

Eucalyptus oil: On of the most popular essential oils in winter seasons. According to a report from the National Library medicine, it has anti-inflammatory properties and fights against herpes simplex virus. It is generally used to ease pain.

Lemon oil: It is often used in cleaning products and is a natural remedy. It has a pleasant scent that would boost your mood.

lemongrass oil: It is commonly used as a natural antidote that kills bacteria. It typically prevents the growth of fungus in the body.

Orange oil: Much like lemon oil, it is used as a natural cleaning agent in your home.

Rosemary oil: It is typicaly used as a kitchen ingredient but it has healing properties that helps minimize stress and proved to reduce joint inflammation.(National library of medicine,2008)

Bergamot oil: It is customarily blended with earl grey tea. It is proven to help lower blood pressure and reduce anxiety.

Cedarwood oil: It is a sought-after ingredient in shampoos and deodorants; it helps with insomnia and anxiety levels.
